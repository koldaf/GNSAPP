Digital Library

The attributes of a digital library includes functions of collection, organization, preservation, access and economy. Notable features include digitalization of materials and metadata but it is obvious that electronic dowments are not localized in them. They have telecommunication connections. Electronic materials are therefore flexible, easy to revise, rearrange and reformatted and can combine with other documents. Such collection is not as bulky as the hard copy.
